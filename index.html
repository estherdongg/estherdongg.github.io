Esther S. Dong

Jazz Composer | Music Technologist

Esther Shengwen Dong is a half-Chinese, half-Korean jazz composer, erhu player, and music technologist. Currently studying jazz composition and creative coding at Berklee College of Music in Boston, USA. She aims to blend the artistry of East and West through her performances and compositions.

She is also passionate about bridging music and technology, she encourages human creativity by exploring new possibilities in sound. Her research interests include music and brain cognition, focusing on how music affects the brain and human emotions. In the field of Music Information Retrieval (MIR), her interests include genre classification, audio tagging, music recommendation systems, pitch detection and tuning, along with the development of DDSP-VST for traditional instruments.

In her free time, she enjoys running, cycling, surfing(riding real ocean waves), reading, and writing.
