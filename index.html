Esther Dong

Composer | Arranger | Erhu Player | Sound Artist

Esther Dong is a half-Chinese, half-Korean jazz composer, arranger, multi-instrumentalist, and sound artist. She holds a Bachelor of Music degree from Berklee College of Music, with a major in Jazz Composition and a minor in Creative Coding, where she also studied jazz Erhu performance under Grammy-nominated jazz violinist Sara Caswell. She has had the privilege of performing with Grammy-winning musicians such as Edward Perez, Mike Block, and Wu Man. Her works have been recognized by Marcus Printup, trumpeter for Jazz at Lincoln Center Orchestra.

She is also passionate about bridging music and technology, she encourages human creativity by exploring new possibilities in sound. She enjoys live coding for music and visual arts.

In her free time, she enjoys running, cycling, surfing(riding the real waves), reading, and writing.
